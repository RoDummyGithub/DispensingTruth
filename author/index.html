AUTHOR

DR. RON GIRARDIN

Facebook Twitter Youtube

I am a member of two of the most rusted professions in our country. I am a licensed pharmacist and an ordained minister. I attended U.S.C., L.I.F.E. Bible College and Talbot Theological Seminary. I’ve been a community pharmacist since 1965 and ordained since 1970.

In the late 1980’s my wife and I moved to the Palm Springs area of Southern California where the ministry of signs and wonders followed. One evening the Lord appeared to me in a dream and informed me I was to write a book regarding His desire to heal. I was sure He was in the wrong bedroom but the result of that dream was this work.

I am still a community pharmacy but over the years I have seen the truths of this book transform broken lives and heal broken bodies, and all the glory goes to Jesus of Nazareth. My wife, of ver 40 years and I currently live in Cathedral City, CA, We have two grown children, a daughter and a son. and 3 lovely granddaughters, the oldest of which is currently attending Concordia University in Chicago.
